CAUTION HOT


CUISINE: Japanese- Chinese Ramen
OPENING HOURS: 11am - 11 pm
LOCATION: TEACHERS VILLAGE QC

Caution Hot is one of the restauranst in Maginhawa that never runs out of customers. We would always have to wait in line because that's how in demand their ramen is! But when you get there, I promise you it is worth the wait. They offer different Ramens, there is only one classic (if you are not the spicy type) then tehre are the spicy ramens and there are levels of spiciness. I also try their favorite milktea here that goes well with the Ramen.

RIB CITY


CUISINE: American
OPENING HOURS: 11:30 am - 10:30 pm
LOCATION: 63 Maginhawa Street, Diliman, QC

Rib City has one of the tastiest ribs I've tasted. You can choose different side dishes for the Suburban steak. It can be the creamy pesto or the mash potato. They serve their ribs in huge portions and the price is very affordable.

Must try: Twin Towers & Suburban RIbs
Must try: Chicken Katsu, Miso Soup, Katsu Curry, Sukiyaki Beef

CRAZY KATSU


CUISINE: Japanese
OPENING HOURS: 11am - 10 pm
LOCATION: 81 Maginhawa Street, Teachers Village, QC

Crazy Katsu is my favorite restaurant in Maginhawa. There was one time where we were here for a week straight. For only P 160, you can get their chicken katsu! It is served with rice and their yummy japanese cabbage. If you are also a fan of spicy sauce, this is the place to come here!

ARTSY CAFE


CUISINE: Cafe-Filipino
OPENING HOURS: 11am - 10 pm
LOCATION: 36 Maginhawa Street, Diliman, QC

Whether it is breakfast or lunch, Artsy Cafe never fail to be the scenty relaxing place to dine in. For breakfast, their crepes are to die for and their pastas and steak are a winner here for lunch. Because its a cafe, there's also a lot of yummy coffee shakes and drinks.

Must try: Fillet Mignon, Mango Crepe, T-Bone Steak
Must try: Roast Beef, Beef Burer, Hungarian Sausage

FAT COUSINS'


CUISINE: American
OPENING HOURS: 11am - 10 pm
LOCATION: 114 Maginhawa Street, Diliman, QC

Fat Cousins' steaks is the best. It also comes with a refillable iced tea and mash potato. What I love here is how well cooked the steak is, it is very tender and juicy while their gravy is also semi-sweet and spicy.

THE LOST BREAD


CUISINE: Cafe, Ice Cream- Desserts
OPENING HOURS: 5pm - 12 midnight
LOCATION: Maginhawa Food Park, Diliman, QC

Lost Bread is where I go to when I crave for desserts at Maginhawa. Their drinks are just out of this world. They are very creative with their different flavors of cotton candy drinks. Their drinks come with chocolates or fruits, etc. I have to admit it is a bit pricy, but the taste is really worth it.

Must try: The Carnival
Must try: Speculoos Shake, Strawberry Cheescake shake

A TASTE OF HEAVEN


CUISINE: Beverages
OPENING HOURS: 12noon - 11 pm
LOCATION: 71 Maginhawa Street, Diliman, QC

Taste of heaven cause when you sip, you literally feel like you're in heaven. A taste of heaven offers different flavors of shake and my personal favorite is their red velvet cheescake.
